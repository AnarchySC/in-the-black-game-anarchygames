IN THE BLACK

// HOW TO PLAY //

// OBJECTIVE

Two ships face off across the arena. Shoot your opponent to score. First to 6 wins. Best of 10 rounds.

[P1] -------- ( shield ) -------- [P2] ^ ^ shoots right shoots left

// CONTROLS

PLAYER 1

W / S or Mouse Wheel — Move

Click or Space — Fire

PLAYER 2

▲ / ▼ — Move

Enter — Fire

Online: Both players use P1 controls (WASD / Mouse / Space). Your inputs are mapped to your ship.

// THE SHIELD

A color-shifting energy orb drifts between the two ships. It blocks all bullets on contact.

The shield bounces off the top and bottom walls and moves side to side unpredictably.

bullets blocked: ---> ( O ) <--- bullets passing: ------> <------ ( O ) (shield elsewhere)

// SHIELD LOCK

When the shield drifts past a player, it locks in place for 2 seconds right in front of their opponent — giving them free cover.

A yellow countdown appears on the shield while locked. Use this time to reposition!

shield passes P1: [P1] ( LOCKED! ) [P2] ^ 2s protection for P2

// MODIFIERS

Random modifiers activate during the match, changing the rules temporarily.

GRAVITY WELL

Alters ship movement speed. DRAG slows you down — SURGE speeds you up. Watch the vignette glow on screen edges.

BLACK HOLE

Appears near the top or bottom edge. Pulls all ships toward it and bends bullet trajectories. Curve your shots around the shield!

OVERCLOCK

Boosts fire rate for both players. When it ends, your weapons malfunction briefly — firing in unpredictable burst patterns.

// SHIP TIERS

Your ship upgrades visually with each point you score. 10 tiers, 3 variants each — randomized every match.

Ships also grow slightly larger with each point — bigger target, but looks cooler.

// ONLINE PLAY

One player clicks HOST ONLINE and gets a 4-character room code. The other clicks JOIN ONLINE and enters the code.

No accounts, no servers — direct peer-to-peer connection.
